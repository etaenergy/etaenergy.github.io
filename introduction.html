Energy Technical Associates

ENERGY TECHNICAL ASSOCIATES (ETA) is a full-fledged elevator company having their corporate office located in ABU DHABI, U.A.E. Established since 2003, with a multitalented team who have rich work experience with all major elevator companies in region for more than three decades. We have successfully installed elevators and escalators for major elevator manufactures like YUSAKI, MITSUBISHI, OTIS, HYUNDAI. We are also providing preventive and predictive maintenance for elevators and escalators with our own fully trained service mechanics. Operating with latest elevator standards of EN-81, and EN-6 standards, ENERGY TECHNICAL ASSOCIATES (ETA) is proud to be working with some of the top developers, builders and property owners in UAE. ENERGY TECHNICAL ASSOCIATES (ETA) is recognized not only for providing value for money but also quality products and responsive services that consistently meet the demands of the UNITED ARAB EMIRATES marketplace. We offer elevator and escalator systems to suit all residential, commercial and high-rise projects.
