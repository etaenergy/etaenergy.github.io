FAQ — Top Elevators in Abu Dhabi

How long does installation take?

Typical passenger MRL installs take 6–12 weeks after approvals and site readiness. Timelines vary by shaft condition and finishes.

Can you modernize without replacing the entire elevator?

Yes. Controller, machine, and door upgrades deliver major gains in safety, ride quality, and energy use with minimal civil work.

What maintenance plans do you offer?

Preventive and full‑care contracts with 24/7 breakdown response, parts coverage options, and digital service logs.
