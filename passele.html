Lift Characteristics

Rated Speed

1.0 m/s to 1.75 m/s

Load Capacity

450kg to 1000kg

Drive System

  • Gearless Traction Motor

  • VVVF Inverter Controlled

Control System

  • Microprocessor-based Controller

  • Simplex or Group Control

Door Type

  • Automatic Center Opening

  • Available in Stainless Steel Finish

Safety Features

  • Overload Sensor

  • Emergency Rescue Device (ARD)

  • Infrared Door Sensor

Sensors and Options

Mobility

Cabin position tracking via magnetic/encoder sensors

Sensors

  • Infrared Door Sensor (Curtain Sensor)

  • Overload Sensor

  • Leveling Sensors

  • Limit Switches (Top & Bottom)

Control

  • PLC/Microcontroller Based Logic

  • Touch Display or Push Button Panel

  • Remote Monitoring via GSM/IoT

Additional Options

  • Voice Announcer and Floor Indicator

  • Automatic Rescue Device (ARD) in power failure

  • WiFi-based Service Diagnostics

Lift Physical & Electrical Properties

Materials

  • Mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum composites

  • Powder-coated cabin and shaft components

Energy Efficiency

Compliant with VDI 4707 and ISO 25745

Power Consumption

  • Typical: 2–5 kW depending on motor rating

  • Standby mode: < 100W with power-saving controller

Voltage & Supply

  • 3-phase, 400V AC, 50Hz (main motor)

  • 24V DC for control and auxiliary circuits

Longevity and Service

  • High-durability components with expected life cycles exceeding 15 years with regular maintenance

  • Remote diagnostics and firmware updates for control panels

  • Modular design enables fast replacement of parts and components by certified technicians

Manufacturing Quality

Engineered and assembled with European quality standards

Warranty

Standard 2 Years (Extendable up to 5 Years)
